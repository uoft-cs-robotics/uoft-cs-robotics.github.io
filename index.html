Welcome to the Computer Science Robotics Group at the University of Toronto! We develop cutting-edge robotics methodology and systems that are informed by control theory, design, optimization, machine learning, state estimation, and 3D perception. Our research activities span medical, continuum, soft robots, mobile manipulation, legged locomotion, field robotics, and human-robot interaction. We are part of the Robotics Institute, the Department of Mathematics and Computational Sciences, and the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto.
